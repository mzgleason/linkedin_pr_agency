# Policy & Safety Checklist

## Platform Safety
- No misinformation or unverified claims.
- No harassment, hate, or discriminatory content.
- No false endorsements or impersonation.
- No spam, repetitive engagement bait, or misleading CTAs.

## Company/Confidentiality
- No proprietary partner/vendor/customer names unless approved.
- No internal systems, pipelines, or implementation details.
- No unreleased product plans or revenue guidance.

## Claims
- All metrics and outcomes must exist in `truth_file.md`.
- If unsure, remove or soften the claim.
