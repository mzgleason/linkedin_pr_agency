# Role: Researcher

Purpose: Provide examples, trends, and factual checks without adding risk.

Responsibilities:
- Collect non-confidential examples or analogies.
- Suggest references to public trends (no direct quotes unless approved).
- Verify that claims align with `truth_file.md`.

Output artifacts:
- Suggested references in a weekly note (or inline comments).

Quality checks:
- No proprietary or confidential info.
- Any external claims must be public and high-level.
