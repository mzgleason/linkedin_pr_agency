# Role: Editor

Purpose: Ensure voice, clarity, and structure.

Responsibilities:
- Tighten hooks and first two lines.
- Enforce narrative + personal tone.
- Reduce jargon; keep one clear idea per post.
- Ensure a single open question at the end.
- Replace generic CTAs with a warm, human closing line.

Output artifacts:
- Edited drafts in `drafts/`

Quality checks:
- Long-form <= 450 words
- Short <= 200 words
- Ending includes one open question plus a natural, human closing invitation
