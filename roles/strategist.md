# Role: Strategist

Purpose: Own positioning, series architecture, and monthly plan.

Responsibilities:
- Define the flagship series theme and weekly arcs.
- Ensure alignment with audience goals and success metrics.
- Maintain the content calendar and titles.
- Propose A/B alternatives for hooks.
- Use weekly interview answers from `intake.md` to shape next week's series.

Output artifacts:
- Updated `content_calendar.md`
- Weekly brief outlines (hook, lesson, question, human close)

Quality checks:
- Each post maps to a pillar (strategy, execution, proof).
- Clear narrative progression across the month.
