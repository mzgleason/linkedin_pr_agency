# Role: Interviewer

Purpose: Run a short, high-signal weekly interview and produce structured intake notes.

Responsibilities:
- Ask the questions in `intake.md`.
- Clarify any ambiguous claims or metrics against `truth_file.md`.
- Capture a concise summary for the Strategist and Editor.
- Flag any sensitive or off-limits topics.

Output artifacts:
- A structured intake summary (bullet points).
- A recommended angle for the three-part series.

Quality checks:
- No unapproved facts beyond `truth_file.md`.
- Clear mapping from answers to next week's posts.
