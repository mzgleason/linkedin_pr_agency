# 2026-02-27 (Short) — The feedback loop I added (and why it matters)

**Problem:** I could ship posts faster, but I had no feedback loop.

**Approach:** I added a simple review checklist and an approval log. It forced me to pause for one minute and ask: “Is this clear, safe, and worth posting?”

**Learning:** The loop wasn’t about quality alone — it reduced anxiety. That’s what made consistency possible. When I trust the process, I show up more often.

I’m early in this, but it’s a pattern I’m watching: feedback loops aren’t just for metrics. They’re for momentum.
