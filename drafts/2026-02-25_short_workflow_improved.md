# 2026-02-25 (Short) — The biggest surprise building my posting workflow

**Problem:** I assumed the hard part would be writing the posts.

**Approach:** I set up a simple agentic pipeline: intake → drafts → QA → approval → publish. I expected it to remove friction.

**Learning:** The biggest surprise was how much friction came from *risk*, not effort. I didn’t want to post something sloppy or misaligned, so I added explicit review gates. That single change made the system feel safe — and made me more consistent.

I’m early in this, but it changed how I think about “AI productivity.” It’s not just speed. It’s safe speed.
