# When a simple workflow break becomes a teaching moment

Quick update from last week: my PR agency workflow failed on Friday and sent two of three posts early when an automated reset triggered. It wasn’t glamorous, but it was useful — debugging the failure showed where assumptions lived in the flow and helped me harden the process so all three posts will publish this week. Small failures like that are high-leverage learning opportunities when you treat them as data rather than shame.
